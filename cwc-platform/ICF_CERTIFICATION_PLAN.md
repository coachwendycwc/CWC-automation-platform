# ICF Certification Plan: ACC to PCC

## Overview

This document outlines the pathway from **ACC (Associate Certified Coach)** to **PCC (Professional Certified Coach)** certification through the International Coaching Federation (ICF).

- **Total Coaching Hours Logged**: Check `/icf-tracker` dashboard
- **Paid Hours**: Track via billing_type = 'paid'

## ACC (Associate Certified Coach) Requirements

| Requirement | Details | Status |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Coach Training** | 60+ hours of ICF-accredited coach-specific training | [ ] |
| **Coaching Experience** | 100+ hours total (75+ paid, 25 can be pro bono) | [ ] |
| **Recent Hours** | 25 hours within 18 months of application | [ ] |
| **Client Diversity** | Minimum 8 different clients | [ ] |
| **Mentor Coaching** | 10 hours over minimum 3 months | [ ] |
| **- Individual Sessions** | Minimum 3 hours 1:1 | [ ] |
| **- Group Sessions** | Maximum 7 hours group | [ ] |
| **ICF Credentialing Exam** | Pass the ACC exam | [ ] |
| **Performance Evaluation** | Complete assessment | [ ] |

### ACC Application Paths

1. **Level 1 Path** (Recommended)
   - Complete ICF Level 1 accredited program (includes training + mentor coaching)
   - Log 100+ coaching hours
   - Pass ICF exam

2. **Portfolio Path**
   - Compile education from various sources totaling 60+ hours
   - Submit recorded coaching sessions
   - Demonstrate ICF Core Competency adherence
   - Log 100+ coaching hours
   - Pass ICF exam

## PCC (Professional Certified Coach) Requirements

| Requirement | Details | Status |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Coach Training** | 125+ hours of ICF-accredited training | [ ] |
| **Coaching Experience** | 500+ hours total (450+ paid) | [ ] |
| **Recent Hours** | 50 hours within 18 months of application | [ ] |
| **Client Diversity** | Minimum 25 different clients | [ ] |
| **Mentor Coaching** | 10 hours with PCC or MCC mentor | [ ] |
| **- Individual Sessions** | Minimum 3 hours 1:1 | [ ] |
| **- Group Sessions** | Maximum 7 hours group | [ ] |
| **ICF Credentialing Exam** | Pass the PCC exam (retake required even if ACC passed) | [ ] |

### PCC Application Paths

1. **Level 2 Path** (Recommended)
   - Complete ICF Level 2 accredited program (125-175 hours)
   - Mentor coaching included in program
   - Log 500+ coaching hours
   - Pass ICF exam

2. **Portfolio Path**
   - Compile 125+ hours from accredited sources
   - Submit recorded sessions demonstrating PCC-level competencies
   - 10 hours mentor coaching with PCC/MCC coach
   - Log 500+ coaching hours
   - Pass ICF exam

## Action Plan

### Phase 1: ACC Certification

#### Step 1: Complete Coach Training (60+ hours)
- [ ] Research ICF Level 1 accredited programs
- [ ] Enroll in chosen program
- [ ] Complete all training modules
- [ ] Obtain certificate of completion

**Recommended Programs:**
- Coach Training EDU
- Co-Active Training Institute (CTI)
- Institute for Professional Excellence in Coaching (iPEC)
- Center for Coaching Certification

#### Step 2: Accumulate Coaching Hours (100+ hours)
- [ ] Track all sessions in ICF Hours Tracker
- [ ] Ensure 75+ hours are paid engagements
- [ ] Work with 8+ different clients
- [ ] Log 25 hours within 18 months of application

**Hour Tracking Tips:**
- Use CWC Platform's ICF Tracker at `/icf-tracker`
- Record: date, client, duration, session type, payment type
- Keep session notes for documentation

#### Step 3: Complete Mentor Coaching (10 hours)
- [ ] Find ICF-credentialed mentor coach (ACC, PCC, or MCC)
- [ ] Schedule sessions over 3+ month period
- [ ] Complete 3+ individual hours
- [ ] Complete up to 7 group hours
- [ ] Obtain mentor coaching completion documentation

**What to Cover:**
- ICF Core Competencies review
- Recorded session feedback
- Ethics and standards
- Exam preparation

#### Step 4: Pass ICF Credentialing Exam
- [ ] Review ICF Core Competencies
- [ ] Study ICF Code of Ethics
- [ ] Take practice exams
- [ ] Schedule exam through ICF
- [ ] Pass with required score

#### Step 5: Submit ACC Application
- [ ] Create ICF account
- [ ] Compile all documentation
- [ ] Submit application fee (~$175 members, ~$325 non-members)
- [ ] Await approval

---

### Phase 2: PCC Certification

#### Step 1: Additional Training (65+ more hours to reach 125)
- [ ] Enroll in ICF Level 2 program OR
- [ ] Complete additional accredited training hours
- [ ] Focus on advanced coaching skills
- [ ] Obtain documentation

#### Step 2: Accumulate Additional Hours (400+ more to reach 500)
- [ ] Continue tracking in ICF Hours Tracker
- [ ] Ensure 450+ total hours are paid
- [ ] Work with 25+ total clients
- [ ] Log 50 hours within 18 months of application

#### Step 3: PCC-Level Mentor Coaching (10 hours)
- [ ] Find PCC or MCC credentialed mentor (ACC cannot mentor for PCC)
- [ ] Schedule sessions over 3+ month period
- [ ] Complete mentor coaching requirements
- [ ] Focus on PCC-level competency markers

#### Step 4: Pass PCC Credentialing Exam
- [ ] Study for PCC-level exam
- [ ] Review advanced competency markers
- [ ] Take practice exams
- [ ] Pass exam (must retake even if ACC exam was passed)

#### Step 5: Submit PCC Application
- [ ] Update ICF account
- [ ] Compile all documentation
- [ ] Submit application fee (~$300 members, ~$500 non-members)
- [ ] Await approval

## ICF Core Competencies

Master these 8 core competencies for both ACC and PCC:

### A. Foundation
1. **Demonstrates Ethical Practice** - Understanding and applying coaching ethics
2. **Embodies a Coaching Mindset** - Developing and maintaining a mindset that is open, curious, flexible, and client-centered

### B. Co-Creating the Relationship
3. **Establishes and Maintains Agreements** - Partnering with client to create clear agreements
4. **Cultivates Trust and Safety** - Creating a safe, supportive environment

### C. Communicating Effectively
5. **Maintains Presence** - Being fully conscious and present with the client
6. **Listens Actively** - Focusing on what the client is and is not saying
7. **Evokes Awareness** - Facilitating client insight and learning

### D. Cultivating Learning and Growth
8. **Facilitates Client Growth** - Partnering with the client to transform learning into action

## Renewal Requirements

Both ACC and PCC credentials must be renewed every 3 years:

| Requirement | ACC | PCC |
|-------------|-----|-----|
| Continuing Coach Education (CCE) | 40 hours | 40 hours |
| - Core Competency hours | 24 hours | 24 hours |
| - Resource Development | 16 hours | 16 hours |
| Renewal Fee | $175 (member) | $275 (member) |

## Notes

- You can apply for PCC directly without ACC if you meet all requirements
- ACC credential is valid for 3 years before renewal
- Mentor coaching hours for ACC cannot count toward PCC mentor coaching
- Keep detailed records of all coaching sessions for audit purposes
- The CWC Platform ICF Tracker helps maintain accurate hour logs
