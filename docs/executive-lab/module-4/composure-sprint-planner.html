14-Day Composure Sprint Planner

Your Two-Week Commitment

This sprint is designed to build composure through consistent daily practice. Week 1 focuses on awareness—noticing your triggers and recognizing patterns in how you respond. Week 2 moves into strategy—developing and practicing new responses that feel authentic and grounded. Each day includes a specific focus area and a simple checkbox to track your practice. At the end of each week, you'll reflect on what you've learned and what's shifting for you.
