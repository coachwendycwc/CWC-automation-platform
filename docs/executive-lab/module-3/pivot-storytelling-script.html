Pivot Storytelling Script Template

Craft a polished, four-part narrative that positions your career shift as intentional and compelling.

How to Use This Worksheet

Work through each of the four parts in order. Draft freely first, then polish. Your final script should be 60–90 seconds when spoken aloud. Your answers auto-save as you type.

1

The Hook

Open with what excites you about the new direction. Lead with passion, not apology. Never start with why you're leaving—start with why you're moving toward.

Example Hooks

Nonprofit → Corporate: "I'm passionate about scaling impact through business strategy and operational excellence."

Corporate → Government: "I want to apply my strategic leadership skills to drive meaningful public outcomes."

Government → Tech: "I've spent a decade solving complex systems problems—now I want to build the solutions."

WOC Tip

You don't owe anyone an explanation for wanting more. Your ambition is valid. Lead with confidence, not justification.

2

The Bridge

Connect your past experience to the new role. This is where you show that your career shift is a natural evolution, not a random leap.

Example Bridges

Nonprofit → Corporate: "Managing a $5M program with 50+ stakeholders taught me operations at scale—the same skills needed to drive enterprise growth."

Corporate → Government: "Ten years leading cross-functional teams in complex, regulated environments prepared me for the interagency challenges of public service."

3

The Evidence

Share 2–3 specific accomplishments that prove you can deliver in your target context. Use metrics wherever possible. Pull directly from your Module 2 win log.

4

The Vision

Articulate what you'll bring and why your unique perspective matters. This is where you turn the "prove it again" bias on its head—your cross-sector experience is an advantage, not a liability.

Example Visions

Nonprofit → Corporate: "I bring a stakeholder-first lens that most corporate leaders are missing—the ability to drive results while centering the people impacted."

Government → Tech: "I bring user empathy at scale that most product teams lack—I've designed systems that serve millions."

✓

Your Complete Pivot Story

Now assemble all four parts into your polished script. Read it out loud. Time it (aim for 60–90 seconds). Refine until it feels natural.
