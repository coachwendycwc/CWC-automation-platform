2
Meeting Agenda — Know the Arc Before You Walk In
Plan your 30 minutes. Know what happens in each phase.
0–3

Open — Set the tone and state the purpose

Express appreciation. Anchor to the relationship. State your purpose directly, no qualifier.

3–10

Case — Market data → Impact evidence → Ask

Deliver the full four-part script. Don't rush. Let the evidence land before moving to the number.

10–12

Ask — Say the number. Stop talking. Wait.

State the specific number. Close the script. Do not fill the silence. Your patience here is power.

12–25

Respond — Listen, navigate, stay grounded

Use your Objection Response Cards. Stay calm and direct. You don't have to accept anything on the spot.

25–30

Close — Leave with something specific

Regardless of outcome, leave with a follow-up date, a milestone list, or written confirmation agreed.

3
Pre-Meeting Mindset & Checklist
What you bring into the room shapes what happens in it. Complete this the day before.
  • Negotiation Preparation Worksheet complete
  • Market research data ready — I know the range, I don't need to read from a sheet
  • Top 2–3 impact wins memorized with numbers
  • Full script practiced out loud at least 3 times
  • Top 2 objection responses practiced
  • My specific ask confirmed — not a range, a number
  • Walk-away minimum decided (keep private)
  • Meeting is on the calendar
