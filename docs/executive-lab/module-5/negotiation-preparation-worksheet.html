My Four-Part Negotiation Script
Write your full script. Then practice out loud — at least 3 times before the meeting.

Case — Present your market data and impact evidence
Ask — State your specific number. Say it. Stop talking. Wait.
Close — Then be silent

Objection Prep

Readiness Checklist
Check each item before the meeting. Don't skip this step.
  • Market research pulled from at least 3 sources
  • My specific ask number is set — not a range, a number
  • Top 3–5 impact wins documented with numbers
  • Impact summary practiced out loud
  • Full four-part script written out
  • Script practiced out loud at least 3 times
  • Top 2 objection responses prepared and rehearsed
  • Walk-away minimum is decided (keep private)
  • Meeting requested on the calendar
