Mentor vs. Sponsor vs. Advocate Sorter

Most people overcount their sponsors. Sort your network honestly — and decide which relationships to deepen.

Mentor

Talks to you. Gives advice and guidance. Helps you think — but does not move the decision.

Sponsor

Talks about you. “Someone other than your direct manager who actively advocates for your career advancement and/or creates opportunities for you.”Definition: McKinsey & LeanIn, 2025

Advocate / Connector

Opens the network. Vouches for you laterally and connects you to rooms you can't reach alone.

How to use this worksheet

List the people in your network. For each, choose the role they actually play right now — not what you hope. Then mark which relationships you want to deepen and the next step to do it. Answers auto-save as you type.
