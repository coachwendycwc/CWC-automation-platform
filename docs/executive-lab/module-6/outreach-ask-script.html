How to use this worksheet

Pick one real empty seat from your Advisor Map and a real person who could fill it. Build the four parts of your ask, then assemble them into one message you can send. Specific beats vague every time. Answers auto-save as you type.

1
Who & Which Seat
Anchor this to something real from your Advisor Map.
2
Build the Four Parts
Draft each piece separately first. You'll assemble them next.
Example: “We worked together on the Q3 launch, and I've valued your perspective ever since.”
Example: “Would you be willing to put my name forward for the cross-functional lead role?” (not “any advice?”)
Example: “I led the same kind of initiative last year and delivered it 3 weeks early.”
Example: “No pressure at all if the timing isn't right — I'd value your honest read either way.”
3
Assemble Your Message
Put the four parts together in your own voice. Read it out loud once — if it sounds like you, it's ready.
